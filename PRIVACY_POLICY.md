## 7. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

### 7.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser.

### 7.2 How We Use Cookies

**Essential Cookies** (Required):
- Authentication and login
- Session management
- Security features
- **You cannot opt out** (required for Service to function)

**Analytics Cookies** (Optional):
- Usage tracking and analytics
- Performance monitoring
- **You can opt out** (see Section 7.3)

**Preference Cookies** (Optional):
- Remember your settings
- UI preferences
- **You can opt out** by clearing browser data

### 7.3 Managing Cookies
You can control cookies through:
- **Browser Settings**: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies
- **Opt-Out Tools**: Use browser extensions or privacy tools
- **Clear Cookies**: Delete cookies from browser settings

**Warning**: Blocking essential cookies will prevent you from using the Service.

### 7.4 Third-Party Cookies
We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Third-party service providers (like analytics tools) may set their own cookies subject to their privacy policies.
